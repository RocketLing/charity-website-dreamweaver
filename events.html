10th Annual Fire Heroes Dinner Gala

The California Fire Foundation hosts an annual gala in Los Angeles to celebrate the courage and commitment of California’s firefighters and raise funds to support the Foundation. The annual gala is attended by supporters, firefighters, and Foundation stakeholders, and the next gala will be announced soon.(...) That is precisely why it is important that you take a moment to click on our Annual Dinner Gala page links to become a sponsor and/or make dinner reservations now to join us along with many sports celebrities, coming together to help others in need.

California Firefighters Memorial

The 19th Annual California Firefighters Memorial Ceremony will be held on September 25, 2021. Ensuring the safety of all friends and family is of utmost importance and more information regarding the ceremony will be available as date approaches.(...) As part of the 2020 California Firefighters Memorial Tribute, names of 32 fallen California firefighters are engraved on the California Firefighters Memorial Wall at California State Capitol Park, Sacramento. In addition to the commemorative video tribute, all 32 fallen heroes from 2020 will be honored at the 2021 Memorial Ceremony.

Firefighter Appreciation Month

Firefighter Appreciation Month raises funds and awareness to support firefighters, their families, and the communities they protect. The California State Senate has deemed the month of September as Firefighters Appreciation Month — a month to honor, pay respect to, and celebrate these extraordinary men and women.(...) Businesses across the state will participate in Firefighter Appreciation Month by raising funds and awareness for the California Fire Foundation! Your business can join in – consider:
Committing a certain percentage of sales on a specific day in September to benefit the California Fire Foundation.
Committing 100% of sales of a specialty ‘Firefighter Appreciation’ item for the month of September to benefit the California Fire Foundation.
